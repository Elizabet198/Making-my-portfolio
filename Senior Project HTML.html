Creating my portfolio

For my senior project, I created a personal portfolio website from scratch to showcase my skills, projects, and certifications as a student at a technical high school. I designed and coded the entire site using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, with a strong focus on clean structure, responsive design, and user-friendly navigation. I organized the project using separate files for each section—such as hobbies, certifications, and projects—to maintain clarity and scalability. The site features modern design elements like Google Fonts, Ionicons for icons, animated background effects, and a responsive layout that adapts to different screen sizes. I also used GitHub Pages to publish the site, allowing it to be live and accessible online. This project demonstrates not only my technical knowledge in web development but also my ability to manage a full project from planning to deployment.

Speed Car

For this project, I built a VEX EXP Speed Bot robot, focusing on creating a high-performance design optimized for speed. The challenge was to develop a robot that could quickly and efficiently complete tasks while maintaining precision and stability. I used the VEX EXP system to construct the robot, integrating various components such as motors, sensors, and structural parts to maximize its performance. Through rigorous testing and iteration, I fine-tuned the robot’s speed and handling to meet the project’s requirements. This hands-on experience enhanced my understanding of robotics, engineering principles, and problem-solving strategies.

Skills USA pin design

For my Skills USA competition, I created a custom pin design that highlighted the essence of Connecticut. My first design incorporated blue tones to symbolize the state, with a robin and mountain laurel flowers representing local wildlife. However, I wanted to make the design more dynamic and engaging, so I reimagined it with a cartoon style, reflecting Connecticut's vibrant fall colors. The design features the state bird flying across the sky, framed within the outline of Connecticut, with a sign at the bottom to hang from the pin.

I used Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator to bring this vision to life, and I was honored to have my design reach both regional and national levels, though I did not progress further.

Skills USA shirt design

For this project, I was tasked with creating a custom logo for my school’s Skills USA team, intended to be printed on t-shirts for competition. My goal was to develop a design that not only captured the spirit of Skills USA but also reflected the unique identity of our school. I started by brainstorming concepts that would be both visually striking and represent our team’s values of skill, teamwork, and determination. After refining my ideas, I created a bold and modern logo that would stand out on apparel while maintaining a professional yet energetic look. This project allowed me to enhance my graphic design skills and my ability to communicate a strong visual message.
